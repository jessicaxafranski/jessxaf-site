MY ROLE

As Product Manager at Bridge, a key technology partner in Brazil's internationally recognized public healthcare system (SUS), I collaborated with government health agencies and medical institutions to develop unified electronic health records. Working alongside engineers, clinicians, and policymakers, I balanced compliance with HL7 Brasil standards and user-centered design principles to enhance data infrastructure for 210+ million citizens.

EVOLUTION (ORIGIN STORY)

The healthcare sector faced critical gaps:
  • Data Fragmentation: Siloed health records hindered pandemic response efforts (e.g., delayed COVID-19 test result sharing).
  • Accessibility Barriers: Clinicians struggled with outdated systems, leading to 30%+ manual data entry errors.

METRICS

  • 20% increase in data accuracy: Streamlined health data management workflows.
  • 40% faster adoption of electronic records: Usability improvements and stakeholder training programs.
  • National Health Data Network integration: Enabled real-time access to vaccination data for 10M+ citizens.
  • 100% compliance with gender identity policies: Inclusive system redesign.
