Americanas Marketplace

MY ROLE

At Americanas Marketplace, one of Latin America's largest e-commerce platforms, I led two critical product initiatives as a Product Manager. Partnering with engineers, designers, sales, and marketing teams, I reimagined the retailer experience-transforming disjointed order tracking systems into seamless tools and rebuilding onboarding flows that empowered new sellers.

My days blurred between strategy sessions with executives, user interviews with overwhelmed retailers, sprint planning with developers, and campaign alignment with marketing. Collaborating with sales teams, we identified high-impact features to drive platform adoption, while marketing amplified seller success stories through tailored narratives. Together, we streamlined complex workflows, integrated real-time logistics data, and crafted tools that resonated with both technical and business needs.
