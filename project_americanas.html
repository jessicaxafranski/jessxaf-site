Americanas Marketplace

MY ROLE

At Americanas Marketplace, one of Brazil’s leading e-commerce platforms, I led product initiatives focused on seller experience. Working with engineering, design, operations, and go-to-market teams, I helped optimize seller workflows, reduce friction in onboarding, and bring more transparency to order tracking.

My work balanced strategic planning, user research, and hands-on delivery – always connecting data, stakeholder needs, and technical constraints to ship solutions that scaled with the business.

EVOLUTION (ORIGIN STORY)

  • Post-purchase uncertainty: Sellers had limited visibility into order status, generating confusion and repetitive support tickets.
  • Onboarding bottlenecks: Complex registration and compliance flows slowed down new seller activation and marketplace expansion.

METRICS

  • 30% fewer support tickets: Clear order timelines and statuses reduced “where is my order?” inquiries.
  • 35% seller growth: Simplified onboarding attracted a broader mix of SMBs and enterprise partners.
  • 15% faster order processing: Unified infrastructure enabled real-time updates and fewer manual interventions.
  • 10% activation efficiency gain: Streamlined compliance workflows cut back-office effort.

KEY TAKEAWAYS

  • Plain-language communication: Reframing order statuses in simple terms reduced confusion for non-technical sellers.
  • Embedded compliance: Automating validations inside the product experience lowered operational risk and workload.
  • Segmented flows: Tailoring journeys for different seller profiles (SMBs vs. enterprises) improved both speed and quality of activation.

STRATEGIC IMPACT

By addressing visibility and onboarding gaps, we strengthened marketplace liquidity and seller trust, while building foundations for scalable growth.

  • Boosted platform liquidity: Better order transparency encouraged repeat usage and higher transaction volume.
  • Operationalized compliance: Automated checks reduced regulatory and operational risk.
  • More resilient growth: Improved seller experience supported sustainable expansion across seller segments.
